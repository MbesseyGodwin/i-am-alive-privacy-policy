About I Am Alive

I Am Alive is a personal safety and emergency alert application designed to help users stay connected with trusted contacts. If a user fails to check in or triggers an emergency alert, the app automatically notifies their emergency contacts with relevant information including location details.

The app is designed for individuals, travelers, families, and workers who want an extra layer of safety and peace of mind.

  • Daily safety check-ins
  • Automatic missed check-in alerts
  • Emergency SOS alerts
  • Location sharing during incidents
  • Trusted emergency contact notifications

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and only collect data required to provide safety services.

  • Email and phone number for account creation
  • Emergency contact information
  • Location data during check-ins and SOS events
  • Device information for reliability

Location data helps emergency contacts identify your last known position during emergencies. Users can control or disable location tracking in settings.

We do not sell personal data.

The free version of the app may display advertisements through Google AdMob.
