I Am Alive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

I Am Alive ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the I Am Alive mobile application.

The application is designed to help users stay safe by providing check-in reminders, emergency alerts, and location-based safety notifications to trusted contacts.

By using the I Am Alive application, you agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information in order to provide our services effectively.

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (securely encrypted)

Emergency Contact Information

Users can add trusted contacts who may be notified during emergencies or missed check-ins.

  • Contact name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Notification preferences

Location Information

Location data may be collected in order to provide emergency services and safety alerts.

Location may be collected when:

  • You perform a safety check-in
  • You trigger an SOS alert
  • Background location tracking is enabled

Location information may include:

  • Latitude and longitude
  • GPS accuracy
  • Speed and heading
  • Approximate address

This information helps emergency contacts identify your last known location in case of danger.

Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Network type
  • Battery level (during SOS events)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve the I Am Alive service.

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable safety check-ins
  • Notify emergency contacts when check-ins are missed
  • Send SOS alerts during emergencies
  • Provide last known location during incidents
  • Improve system reliability and security
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Advertising

The free version of the app may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect advertising identifiers or device information in order to deliver relevant ads.

Users who subscribe to premium plans may receive an ad-free experience.

For more details please review Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Payments

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed securely through Flutterwave.

We do not store full payment card information on our servers. All payment data is processed by Flutterwave according to their own privacy and security policies.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information including:

  • Secure password encryption
  • HTTPS encrypted communication
  • Authentication tokens for secure sessions
  • Access controls for sensitive data
  • Audit logging of important actions

While we strive to protect your information, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services.

  • Account data is retained while the account remains active
  • Location data may be automatically deleted after a retention period
  • Incident reports may be stored for moderation and safety review

Users may request deletion of their account and personal data.

8. User Control

Users have control over their personal data through application settings.

  • Enable or disable location tracking
  • Manage emergency contacts
  • Update account information
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Request account deletion

9. Children's Privacy

I Am Alive is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is discovered, it will be removed immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

The application relies on trusted service providers to deliver core functionality.

  • Expo Push Notifications
  • Africa's Talking (SMS alerts)
  • Brevo (email notifications)
  • Flutterwave (payments)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)

These providers may process limited information necessary to perform their services.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates may occur due to new features, legal requirements, or improvements to our service.

The most recent version will always be available on this page.
