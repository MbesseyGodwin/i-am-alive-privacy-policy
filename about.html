About I Am Alive

Our Mission

I Am Alive is a personal safety and emergency response app designed to help you stay safe and connected with your loved ones. Our goal is to make sure help can reach you quickly when it matters most.

What the App Does

I Am Alive provides several powerful safety features:

  • Daily Check-ins: Set a daily check-in time. If you miss a check-in, your emergency contacts are automatically notified.
  • SOS Alerts: One-tap SOS sends your location and alerts your emergency contacts immediately.
  • Location Tracking: Share your location in real-time during emergencies or check-ins.
  • Emergency Contacts: Notify family, friends, or caregivers if you’re in danger or need assistance.
  • Reports & Incident Logging: Keep track of incidents or emergencies safely and securely.
  • KYC & Health Info: Add critical health information for better support in emergencies.
  • Subscriptions & Premium Features: Upgrade to unlock unlimited emergency contacts, SMS/WhatsApp alerts, and an ad-free experience.

Why Choose I Am Alive?

With a simple interface, reliable notifications, and comprehensive safety tools, I Am Alive empowers you to stay safe wherever you are. Your peace of mind is our top priority.
